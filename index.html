Company profile

公司简介

KICHI art consulting is a professional international art consulting and brokerage firm. Founded in 2008, the company is headquartered in New York. The company's main business includes art consulting, art broker, artist recommendation, art exhibition planning, art activity preparation organization and so on. China branch is located in Beijing and Nanjing.

KICHI艺术咨询公司是一家专业的国际艺术咨询和经纪公司，成立于2008年， 公司总部设在纽约。公司主要运营业务包括艺术咨询，艺术品经纪，艺术家推荐，艺术展览策划，艺术活动筹备组织等。中国国内公司设在北京及南京。
